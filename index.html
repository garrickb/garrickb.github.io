Typing Game

This web game was created just for fun. It's unfinished and very raw, but it still conveys the basic game play mechanic that I imagined. It's a typing RPG game. I wanted it to be a dungeon crawler that allowed the player to explore a dungeon, level up, gather loot, and upgrade their equipment. The only way to kill enemies is to type the word assigned to them.

Ludum Dare 28

This web game was developed in 48 hours for the Ludum Dare 28 online competition. People around the world participated in the game development competition that centered around one given theme. The theme was 'You Only Get One'. My game follows a dog as he runs from pet store to pet store getting the free treats that they are giving out, and after all, the dog only gets one per store.

Kitchen Support

Kitchen Support is a web and mobile application that allows users to better utilize the ingredients they have in their kitchen. During the development of this project we attempted to follow as many industry standards as we could. We have code reviews for every pull request, good coding standards and commenting , Jira for product management, and CircleCI for continuous interation. I am serving as the scum master for the team as we follow the agile scrum process of software engineering. Our team consists of four people on the mobile side, and two on the web/api side (which is what I am working on).
